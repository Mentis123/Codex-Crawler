AI News Aggregation System Architecture

Search Agent

Scans configured news sources and searches for AI-related content using:

  • Keyword extraction from criteria
  • Web scraping with timeout handling
  • Article validation and filtering
Output: Raw articles and content
↓

Evaluation Agent

Analyzes and scores articles based on:

  • Relevance scoring against criteria
  • Content quality assessment
  • AI topic validation
Output: Scored and ranked articles
↓

Rationale Agent

Generates explanations for article selection:

  • Two-sentence rationales
  • Business context analysis
  • Impact assessment
Output: Articles with rationales
↓

Review Agent

Creates final reports and documentation:

  • PDF report generation
  • CSV export
  • Article archiving
Output: Final reports and exports

System Features:

  • Automated AI news discovery and aggregation
  • Intelligent content evaluation and ranking
  • Detailed rationale generation for selected articles
  • Multiple export formats (PDF, CSV)
  • Configurable search criteria and sources
